Tutorials

You can use defresh in your site's .js files with the defresh() function:

location.href = '/page';
defresh('/page', 'push');

location.replace('/page');
defresh('/page', 'replace');

document.write(this.responseText);
defresh('/page', 'none');

About

Defresh was launched in 2020, using a mixture of AJAX and the History API. Defresh was created for programmers who wanted faster links on their sites. Defresh is completely free to use and share.
